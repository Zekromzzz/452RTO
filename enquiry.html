Fish Education Courses offered

ICT60220

This qualification reflects the role of individuals with extensive experience in professional technical skills or managing business and personnel management skills in various information and communication technology (ICT) roles. Individuals who assume these roles perform complex tasks in their professional fields, work independently, and lead teams or businesses in strategic directions. They apply their skills to a wide range of industries and business functions, or as business owners (self-employed/contractors)s

ICT20120

This qualification certificate is designed specifically for those who develop the necessary digital and technical skills to prepare for work. These people perform a series of basic programs and operational tasks that require numerical and technical skills. They use limited practical skills and knowledge to perform a series of primarily daily tasks in a defined environment. This qualification is suitable for individuals who typically perform under direct supervision.

UEE21720

This qualification provides the ability to collect/receive and store inventory in the workplace; Set up and store equipment and tools; Assist in installation, troubleshooting, maintenance, and repair activities.

ICT20219

This is an entry-level qualification for telecommunications linesworkers working on the construction and maintenance of high-speed national broadband network (NBN) infrastructure.

ICT30419

This qualification is for telecommunications operators working on the construction and maintenance of high-speed national broadband network (NBN) infrastructure, and on required technology. Telecommunications technology operators may: perform equipment and system installation work on a customer network to enable efficient access and interconnection to the NBN services, including connecting and jointing of copper cable, diagnosing and rectifying copper cable system faults, and migrating.
